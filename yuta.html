Okkotsu Yuta

Biography

It is believed at (0.1) that Rika is a spirit attached to Yuta, or rather that he’s cursed BY Rika, but at the end of Volume 0 it is revealed that Yuta was the one who cursed Rika. Yuta is a sorcerer, as well as being a cursed person. Treasure an ‘engagement ring’ given to him by Rika when they were children. A distant descendant of Michizane Sugawara.

Personality

Yuta is commonly downtrodden and or emotional. He lacks self-confidence at the beggining of 0.1, however the arch of Volume 0 is his journey in finding love for himself.

Satoru Gojo

Biography

A former student, now mentor and teacher at Jujutsu High. A distant descendant of Michizane Sugawara, and considered the superman of jujutsu society. Before Suguru Geto's seperation from Jujutsu High, he and Gojo were best friends. Principal Yaga was his teacher when he was a student.

Personality

Satoru Gojo is whimsical when he wishes and serious if he need be. His origin and powers are shrouded in mystery. He can do most anything he tries, so he really doesn't get involved. He says it's 'all for the future generation'.

Itadori Yuji

Biography

A highschooler at Sugisawa Municipal High School #3, Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture and member of the occult research club. Yuji possesses abnormal, superhuman strength even as a non-sorcerer. He is shown to shatter the japanese shot put record, opting to throw the shot put like a baseball. Yuji visits his grandfather in the hospital most days after school. Not much is known about their relationship, but Yuji’s grandfather encourages Yuji to help people. This is Yuji’s final lesson from a member of his blood family, as his grandfather would die peacefully moments later. Yuji is confronted by Fushiguro Megumi for having moved the talisman placed at his school, the mummified finger of Ryomen Sukuna.

Personality

He has a lighthearted and boisterous demeanor, giving many people an immediate impression of kindness.

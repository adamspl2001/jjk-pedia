Cursed Speech

About

An advanced technique passed down from generation to generation in the Inumaki Family. The user imbues their cursed energy into their words causing whatever hears them to obey the command. Heavily reliant on the user having more cursed energy available than the target, and in cases where this is not true the technique has been known to backfire on the user; must be used in moderation as it is seen to be very damaging to the vocal cords of the user. First seen in action by Inumaki Toge in Chapter 0.2 during his first assignment with Yuta Okkotsu at the Hapina Shopping Center.
